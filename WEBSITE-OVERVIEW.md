# Sri Gujarati Vidyalaya — Website Overview

*A summary of the new school website, prepared for the Principal's review.*
*Established 1869 · Mananchira, Kozhikode*

---

## What this is

A modern, mobile-friendly website for the school, with a **private admin panel**
so school staff can update content themselves — no developer needed. Everything
visitors see (parents, students, alumni, job-seekers) can be managed in-house.

---

## Public pages (what visitors see)

| Page | What it shows |
|------|---------------|
| **Home** | Welcome, school highlights, key information at a glance |
| **Heritage** | The school's history and legacy since 1869 |
| **Academics** | Curriculum, streams, and what we teach |
| **Faculty** | Our teachers (currently the school's real 18 teachers) |
| **Admissions** | How to apply, requirements, and the admission process |
| **Campus / Gallery** | Photos (and videos) of campus life and events |
| **Alumni** | Notable past students and alumni community |
| **Careers** | Current job openings, with a dedicated page per job |
| **Contact** | Address, phone, email, and an enquiry form |

---

## Key features

- **Works on all devices** — phones, tablets, and computers, with a layout that
  adjusts automatically.
- **Multi-language** — visitors can read the site in **English, Malayalam,
  Gujarati, Hindi, and Tamil** with one tap.
- **WhatsApp quick button** — a floating button lets visitors message the school
  instantly.
- **News & Events** — announcements and events can be posted and shown on the site.
- **Job listings & careers** — openings can be posted; each job has its own page
  that looks good when shared on LinkedIn or WhatsApp.
- **Photo & video gallery** — campus and event media in one place.
- **Contact / enquiry form** — visitors can reach out directly.

---

## Admin panel (staff-only, private)

A secure login at **/admin** lets school staff manage the site without touching
any code. Every action has plain-language guidance and friendly error messages.

Staff can manage:

- **News & Events** — post announcements, attach a photo, publish/unpublish
- **Faculty** — add/edit teachers and their photos
- **Hiring / Jobs** — post and manage job openings
- **Alumni** — feature notable past students
- **Gallery** — upload campus photos and videos

Access is protected by a single secure admin login.
